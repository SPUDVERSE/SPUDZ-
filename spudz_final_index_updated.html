What is SPUDZ?

SPUDZ is a community-driven meme token powering the SPUDVERSE ecosystem on IGRA. The project combines potato parody art, NFT collections, staking rewards, liquidity participation, and active community campaigns into one growing on-chain brand.

SPUDZ started with original art and community culture, then expanded into collectibles, rewards, DeFi activity, and upcoming hand-drawn SNESpudz.
